Do you want to look and feel classy?

Basil Neckties is a classic brand of neckties handmade by the finest designers to suit all your fashion needs. Basil Neckties was incorprated in 2016 and have been featured on the streets of Accra in wonderful ways. We made our first presence on the International Market early 2017, when our bow ties were featured at a wedding held at Fairmont The Norfolk Hotel in Nairobi, Kenya. We have clients enjoying our products in the United Kingdom, United States of America and other parts of the world. To hit the nail right on the head, I guess it's safe to say that we are global.
